AI Personal Assistant Agent with Claude

Pre-class setup

Complete this before class so we can spend the workshop building your assistant. It should take about 10 minutes if you already use Google, and a little longer if your email or calendar is somewhere else.

What to bring

  • 1Claude Pro or Max subscription
  • 2Google account
  • 3Laptop with the Claude Desktop app installed and signed in
  • 4Mobile phone with the Claude mobile app installed and signed in

Setup steps

Step 1

Claude Pro or Max subscription
  1. Create or sign in to your Claude account. Go to claude.ai/signup.
  2. Upgrade to Claude Pro or Max. Go to claude.ai/upgrade, choose Pro or Max, and complete checkout. Pro is enough for the workshop.
Step 2

Install Claude Desktop on your laptop and sign in
  1. Download Claude Desktop. Go to claude.com/download and choose macOS or Windows.
  2. Install and open it. On Mac, open Claude from Applications. On Windows, open Claude from the Start menu.
  3. Sign in with the same Claude account. Send a quick hello message to confirm the app works.

Claude Desktop supports macOS 11 or newer and Windows 10 or newer. If you are on a locked-down work laptop, install early in case your IT policy blocks new apps.

Step 3

Install Claude Mobile on your phone and sign in

Use the same Claude account on mobile so your workshop setup is reachable from your phone.
  1. Install Claude Mobile. Use the iOS App Store or Google Play links from claude.com/download.
  2. Sign in with the same Claude account. Send a quick hello message from your phone.

No command-line tools are required before class. Claude Mobile is the only phone setup you need.

Step 4

Set up the Google account we will use in class

Have a Google account ready, but do not connect it to Claude before class unless it was already connected.

If your primary email and calendar are Google, then you are done. Bring that login to class.

If you use iCloud, Outlook, Yahoo, or another provider, create a free Google account at accounts.google.com/signup. We will use Google for class exercises and discuss connection choices together. You do not need to migrate your life into Google.

If your email or calendar lives in iCloud, Outlook, or Yahoo, keep the provider setup reference handy. We will use it in class if you need provider-specific forwarding or calendar steps.

!

Privacy & safety before you connect anything

  • Do not connect Google, Gmail, or Calendar directly to Claude before class unless you already did and are comfortable keeping it connected.
  • Read access can expose private content: emails, attachments, meeting details, file names, and documents.
  • Write access can change real things: agents may send mail, edit or delete files, create invites, reschedule meetings, or cancel events.
  • Use the smallest permission that works and revoke access after the workshop if you only needed it for testing.

Safety note: Do not forward a sensitive inbox or publish a sensitive calendar just for class. Use sample events or a workshop-only Google account if you are unsure.
